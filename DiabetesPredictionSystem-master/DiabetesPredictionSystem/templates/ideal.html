Parameters:

  • Glucose :
    Normal blood sugar levels are less than 100 mg/dL after not eating (fasting) for at least eight hours. And they're less than 140 mg/dL two hours after eating. During the day, levels tend to be at their lowest just before meals.

  • Blood pressure :
    Ideal blood pressure is considered to be between 90/60mmHg and 120/80mmHg. high blood pressure is considered to be 140/90mmHg or higher. low blood pressure is considered to be 90/60mmHg or lower.

  • Skin thickness :
    Skin thickness is primarily determined by collagen content and is increased in insulin-dependent diabetes mellitus. We measured skin thickness in 66 IDDM patients aged 24–38 yr and investigated whether it correlated with long-term glycemic control and the presence of certain diabetic complications.

  • BMI :
    A BMI of less than 18.5 means that a person is underweight. A BMI of between 18.5 and 24.9 is ideal. A BMI of between 25 and 29.9 is overweight. A BMI over 30 indicates obesity.

  • Diabetes pedigree function :
    It utilizes information from a person’s family history to predict how diabetes will affect that individual.
